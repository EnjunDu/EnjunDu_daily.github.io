# Personal Statement

Computer science and technology is a new professional field that combines mathematics, engineering, and science, and plays an important role in modern technology.

I am an undergraduate student majoring in cybersecurity, which is a branch of computer science. However, I have a deeper interest in the subject of computer science and technology. In order to pursue systematic study of computer science, improve my programming skills, strengthen my ability to write research papers in English and get prepared for a doctoral degree,I began engaging in research starting from the second semester of my sophomore year and have conducted in-depth studies in various computer-related courses. Through unremitting efforts, I obtained remarkable scores in courses such as Linear Algebra, Mathematical Analysis, English for Academic Purposes, C Language Programming, Discrete Mathematics, Introduction to Cryptography, Cryptographic Engineering, Data Structures and Algorithm Design, and ranked among the top of my class.This is a good demonstration of my solid academic foundation and particular strength in computer study. Interest in computer technology is the greatest driver for my study. I counted myself very lucky to choose a major suitable for me. Learning and practical application of computer theories gave full play to my talents and fully tapped my potential. I have an intense passion for and attach great importance to programming, for it is a dialogue with computer and an expression of ideas. I enjoy the process of programming, since it is exceedingly fascinating and challenging.Not only did I master C, Java, and Python, but I also developed a certain understanding of the universality of programming languages, which enables me to quickly learn a new programming language. Additionally, I have a strong grasp of deep learning and machine learning, and I have gained substantial knowledge in large language models (LLMs) and software engineering. This is thanks to my involvement in three research groups—one focused on developing deep learning-based recommendation systems, another on studying the security issues of recommendation systems based on large language models (my undergraduate major in Cyberspace Security has provided me with a unique advantage in researching security topics), and the third on addressing the automatic completion of test oracle problems using large language models. These experiences have provided me with a solid foundation in various computer science research areas during my undergraduate studies.

With more in-depth professional knowledge, I had a better understanding of the computer field while acquiring professional knowledge and technical methods. I believe that theoretical knowledge must be kept pace with practice, so on the basis of grasping theoretical knowledge, I also put in more effort to practice. Well aware that research papers are an effective indicator of a student's research ability, especially for those aspiring to pursue a doctoral degree, I placed great importance on writing research papers. I was fortunate enough to conduct in-depth research and write papers under the guidance of Professors Zhida Qin, Fei Sun, and Hui Liu. With a passion for artificial intelligence and computer science, I chose recommendation algorithms, computer security, and large language models as the focal points of my research. To make the best out of this project, I did a lot of preparatory work—studying the basic theories of machine learning, mastering linear regression, and taking online courses such as **CS231n **Deep Learning and CS224n Natural Language Processing from Stanford University. I also became proficient in models like KNN, CNN, and RNN. Additionally, I delved deeply into various recommendation algorithms, such as those mentioned in papers on LightGCN Simplifying, FMLPrec, Neural Graph Collaborative Filtering, Self-Attentive Sequential Recommendation, and S3-Rec.These efforts have greatly enhanced my confidence in successfully conducting research.

In my research, I have focused on optimizing and improving recommendation systems. In the paper "Behavior Habits Enhanced Intention Learning for Session Based Recommendation," I constructed hypergraphs to capture user behavior habits and combined them with global item transition relationships, significantly enhancing the accuracy of recommendation systems. This research involved data analysis, machine learning, and graph neural networks. Under the guidance of my advisor, I tested the framework's effectiveness on multiple datasets, proving its superiority and enhancing my knowledge in graph neural networks and recommendation algorithms. Additionally, in the paper "CaDDiSR: Context-Aware Dynamic Data Integration and Storage for Recommendations," I designed a dynamic data integration framework that considers the context of user interactions to improve recommendation accuracy. This experience provided me with valuable knowledge in data storage, context-aware computing, and machine learning. In my LLM test oracle research, I analyzed the characteristics of C, Python, and Java languages and explored the possibility of using LLMs to automatically generate assertion statements, with the goal of successfully publishing a CCF-A paper. These research experiences have laid a solid foundation for my future scientific work.

In the coming year, I plan to conduct in-depth research on my current direction. If I am fortunate enough, I hope to join your research group as a research assistant in advance and participate in self-funded offline research internships during winter and summer vacations. With continuous effort and learning throughout my junior and senior years, I believe that by the time I pursue my doctoral studies, my research capabilities will have significantly improved.

With the development of computer science, applications of various functions have been created, which have brought endless joy to our lives. I think computer science is an essential tool for a better life and it can change people all over the world, or help people get involved in a group which can change the world. All of these are challenging and I like new things, so the Computer major suits my character and interest. Hence, I inspire to further my study in the Computer field, so I decide to purchase a doctorate degree of Computer Science in your university.
